About Me


I received my B.S. in Communication Sciences and Disorders from the University of Texas at Austin in 2014. Upon graduating, I decided to travel with my pup, Koda (pictured above), and see everything that makes America so great. While traveling and exploring are true passions of mine, I decided to return to school to pursue a career in coding / web development. I will graduate from the Trilogy Educations Services' Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp (through the University of Texas at Austin) in May 2020.

I have a passion for language: reading, writing, speaking, and foreign languages, as well. Communcations and coding allow me to invest in and outlet my passion in various ways. With my communications degree, I focused on the science behind language aquisition across all spoken languages as well as signed languages. I spent two years devoted to the study of American Sign Language and since my family immigrated from Germany, I spent some time learning German Sign Language, as well, though more for fun than for fluency.

While learning web development and coding, I have delved into the communication surrounding everyday technology. Though sometimes challenging, communication of any kind is critical to every day life, and I truly love exploring all forms of communication. My current goal is to become a Front End Web Developor for a company that allows me to utilize my skills in unique and challenging ways, as I aspire to work hard and push boundaries on new learnings and developments.
